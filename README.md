Overview

Compton Digital Services is a modern, fully interactive web platform designed to help students, professionals, and job seekers access high-quality services quickly and efficiently. The platform offers a variety of services including assignment support (normal, practical, and project-based), LinkedIn profile setup, CV writing, and Windows installation. Each service has a dedicated page with detailed descriptions, pricing, and step-by-step guidance, allowing clients to understand exactly what they are purchasing.

Seamless Booking & Payment

The platform integrates MTN Mobile Money (MoMo) payment options, enabling clients to securely complete transactions online before confirming their bookings. After payment, clients can instantly notify the service provider via WhatsApp, ensuring real-time communication and fast confirmation. This combination of digital payments and instant messaging creates a trustworthy, professional, and efficient booking experience.

Comprehensive Admin Dashboard

Compton Digital Services includes a dashboard for managing all bookings. Administrators can view client details, payment references, service types, and booking statuses in real-time. The dashboard provides complete control over pending, confirmed, and completed requests, ensuring smooth operations and professional service management.

Modern Design & User Experience

The platform is built with responsive, mobile-friendly design principles. Animated service cards, hover effects, smooth scrolling, and visually appealing layouts make navigation intuitive and engaging. Every page is structured to provide clarity, reliability, and a professional look, creating an exceptional user experience for both clients and administrators.
